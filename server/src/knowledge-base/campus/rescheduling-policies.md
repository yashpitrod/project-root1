# Rescheduling & Cancellation Policies

## Rescheduling an Appointment

### How to Reschedule
- Log in to the CampusCare portal using your campus credentials
- Navigate to your **Request History** to view your upcoming appointments
- If your appointment is still in **Pending** status (not yet approved by the doctor), you can cancel it and submit a new request with your preferred time slot
- If your appointment has already been **Approved**, contact the dispensary directly to request rescheduling

### How Far in Advance?
- Rescheduling is easiest at least **2 hours before** your scheduled appointment time
- Last-minute cancellations (within 30 minutes of the appointment) are noted and may affect future appointment priority during busy periods

## Cancelling an Appointment

### When to Cancel
- If you feel better and no longer need to see the doctor
- If you have an unavoidable class conflict or exam
- If you have already visited another healthcare provider

### How to Cancel
- Cancel through the CampusCare portal via Request History
- For pending appointments: simply notify the dispensary so the slot can be given to another student

## What Happens If I Miss My Appointment?

- Missed appointments without cancellation are recorded
- Repeatedly missing appointments (3 or more times without notice) may result in lower scheduling priority
- If you miss an appointment due to an emergency, explain to the dispensary staff — exceptions are always made for genuine cases

## Appointment Confirmation

- Once a doctor **Approves** your request on the portal, you will receive a real-time notification in the CampusCare portal
- Approval means your slot is confirmed — arrive 5 minutes early for registration
- If your request is **Rejected** (e.g., slot is full), you can immediately book a new slot

## Walk-In Policy

- Walk-in patients are welcome during OPD hours, subject to doctor availability
- Walk-in patients may have to wait longer than appointment holders during busy periods
- Emergency walk-ins are always given priority regardless of appointment status
