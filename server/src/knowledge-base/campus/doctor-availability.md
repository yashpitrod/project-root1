# Doctor Availability & Schedules

## General Dispensary Hours

The NITR campus dispensary is open on all working days (Monday to Saturday).

### Weekday Hours (Monday to Friday)
- **Morning OPD**: 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM
- **Afternoon OPD**: 2:00 PM – 5:00 PM
- **Evening Emergency**: 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM (emergency cases only)

### Saturday Hours
- **Morning OPD**: 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM
- Afternoon and evening sessions are closed on Saturday

### Sunday & Public Holidays
- Dispensary is **closed** for regular OPD
- Emergency contact is available through the campus security office (24/7)
- For genuine emergencies on Sundays, call campus security who will contact the on-call doctor

## Available Doctors

The campus dispensary has a rotating team of doctors. Specific doctor availability varies by day.

### How to Check Real-Time Doctor Availability
- The CampusCare portal shows live availability status for each doctor
- A green indicator means the doctor is **Available** for appointments
- An orange/yellow indicator means the doctor is **Busy** (high patient load)
- A red indicator means the doctor is in **Emergency** mode (critical case in progress)

## Appointment Booking
- Students and staff can book appointments through the CampusCare portal
- Select an **available doctor** and a **time slot** during their OPD hours
- Walk-in appointments are also accepted subject to doctor availability
- Time slots are available in 30-minute intervals from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM (excluding lunch break)

## Emergency Medical Contact
- For life-threatening emergencies: call the campus medical emergency line
- Campus security (available 24/7) can assist in reaching emergency medical help
- Students can also go directly to the nearest government hospital for severe emergencies
