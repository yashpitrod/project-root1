# Health Insurance & Medical Referrals

## Campus Health Insurance

### Coverage for Students
- All enrolled students are covered under the campus group health insurance scheme
- Coverage includes: hospitalization, surgical procedures, diagnostic tests, and specialist consultations
- Coverage period: academic year (August to July)

### What Is Covered
- Inpatient hospitalization (minimum 24-hour admission)
- Day-care surgical procedures
- Pre and post-hospitalization expenses (typically 30 days pre and 60 days post)
- Emergency ambulance charges
- Basic diagnostic investigations ordered by a registered doctor

### What Is NOT Covered
- Cosmetic or elective procedures
- Pre-existing conditions (for the first year of enrollment — check policy terms)
- Dental treatment (unless due to accident)
- Vision correction (glasses, contact lenses)
- Medicines purchased without a valid prescription

### How to Make a Claim
1. Obtain a **referral letter** from the campus dispensary doctor
2. Visit the partner hospital or any network hospital
3. For cashless treatment: show your student ID and insurance card at the hospital's insurance desk
4. For reimbursement: submit original bills, prescriptions, and discharge summary to the accounts office within 30 days

## Dispensary Referral Process

### When You Will Be Referred
- When your condition requires specialist care (orthopedic, cardiologist, ENT, etc.)
- When diagnostic tests not available at the dispensary are needed (CT scan, MRI, X-ray)
- When hospitalization is required
- When the dispensary doctor determines your case is beyond primary care

### The Referral Letter
- The referral letter is issued by the dispensary doctor at the time of consultation
- It includes: your diagnosis, treatment given so far, and reason for referral
- The letter entitles you to priority attention at partner hospitals
- Keep the letter with you when you visit the referred specialist

### Partner Hospitals
- The dispensary maintains referral arrangements with the government district hospital and select private hospitals near campus
- Ask the dispensary staff for the current list of partner hospitals

## Sick Leave & Academic Accommodation
- Medical certificates from the campus dispensary are accepted for attendance condonation
- In case of prolonged illness (more than 7 days), a hospitalization summary or specialist letter is required
- Submit the certificate to the Academic Section within 3 days of returning to class
