# Dispensary Timings & Services

## Location
The campus dispensary is located near the main academic block. Look for the red cross signage near the administrative building.

## Services Available at the Dispensary
- General physician consultation (OPD)
- Basic diagnostic tests (blood pressure, blood glucose, temperature, pulse oximetry)
- Dressing and wound care
- Injection and IV fluid administration
- Basic medications dispensed free of charge to registered students and staff
- Referral letters for specialist consultations at partner hospitals
- Sick leave certificate issuance for documented illness

## Medications Available Free of Charge
Common medications available at no cost include:
- Paracetamol (fever and pain)
- Antacids (acidity and heartburn)
- ORS sachets (dehydration)
- Antihistamines (allergy)
- Antiseptic solutions and wound dressings
- Cough syrups and lozenges
- Vitamin supplements (iron, vitamin C, vitamin D)

## Medications Requiring a Prescription
- Antibiotics
- Stronger pain medications (NSAIDs beyond OTC dose)
- Mental health medications
- Specialized treatments

## Lab & Diagnostic Services
- Basic blood tests available through the dispensary referral
- Urine routine microscopy
- Blood sugar testing
- More specialized investigations require referral to the partner hospital

## Referral Network
- The dispensary has a referral arrangement with the government district hospital
- Students requiring specialist care, X-rays, advanced labs, or surgeries are referred with a dispensary letter
- The referral letter ensures priority attention at the referral facility

## Is the Dispensary Open Today?
Check the CampusCare portal for real-time status. If any doctor shows as "Available," the dispensary is open for walk-in and appointment-based consultations.
