# CampusCare Portal — FAQ

## What Is CampusCare?
CampusCare is the digital health management portal for NITR students and staff. It allows you to:
- Book appointments with available doctors
- Receive real-time updates on your appointment status
- Chat with the AI health assistant for symptom triage and FAQ answers
- View your request history
- See live dispensary status and doctor availability

## How Do I Book an Appointment?

1. Log in to CampusCare with your campus email
2. Go to **Student Dashboard**
3. Describe your problem in the health assistant chat
4. Once triage is complete, select an **available doctor** and a **time slot**
5. Submit your request — the doctor will approve or reject it in real-time

## Can I Describe My Problem in Hindi or My Regional Language?
Yes! The CampusCare assistant accepts descriptions in any language. The system will translate and understand your problem automatically.

## How Will I Know If My Appointment Is Approved?
- You will receive a **real-time notification** in the portal the moment the doctor approves or rejects your request
- Check the "Recent Request" card on your dashboard for the current status
- Status options: **Pending** (waiting for doctor), **Approved** (confirmed), **Rejected** (slot unavailable)

## What Does the AI Health Assistant Do?
The AI assistant:
- Asks follow-up questions to understand your symptoms better
- Helps the doctor understand your case faster (triage support)
- Answers questions about dispensary timings, policies, and services
- **It does NOT diagnose you** — it helps the doctor triage your case more efficiently

## Is My Health Information Private?
- Your health information is securely stored and only visible to you and your treating doctor
- The platform uses secure authentication (Firebase) and encrypted communication
- Health data is never shared with other students or unauthorized parties

## What If I Have a Non-Medical Question?
You can ask the assistant questions like:
- "Is the dispensary open today?"
- "How do I reschedule my appointment?"
- "What medicines are free at the dispensary?"
- "How do I submit a sick leave certificate?"

## What If the System Is Down?
- Walk directly to the dispensary during OPD hours
- Contact campus security for after-hours emergencies
- For urgent medical issues, do not wait for the portal — go directly to the dispensary or call emergency services
